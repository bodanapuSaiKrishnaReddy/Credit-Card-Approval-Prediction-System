# Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation

## 💡 Project Concept
**Credit Card Approval Predictor** is a machine learning-powered web application designed for financial institutions and credit analysts to predict the creditworthiness of applicants. By assessing personal, financial, and employment criteria, the application determines whether a client should be approved for a credit card.

## 👥 Target Audience
- **Credit Risk Analysts**: Need a fast, reliable model to pre-screen credit applications.
- **Financial Institutions**: Aim to reduce credit default rates while maximizing customer acquisition.
- **End-user Applicants**: Benefit from a clear, multi-step application form with transparency and instant decisions.

## 🧠 Brainstorming Process
1. **Model Selection**: Evaluated Gradient Boosting, HistGradientBoosting, and Random Forest models. Random Forest was selected for its high robustness and clean feature importance evaluations.
2. **Feature Engineering**: Mapped Kaggle credit record data to binary targets (Approved/Declined) based on historical payment statuses.
3. **Design Philosophy**: Created a professional, corporate-looking interface utilizing an HSL design system to guarantee clean visual aesthetics.
